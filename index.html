SHATTERED COLONY

THE SURVIVORS · HTML5 recreation

How to play

You command the last survivors after the outbreak. Your Headquarters is your first depot. Survivors drive trucks of boards, ammo and food between linked structures.

  • Workshop — place on a building to scavenge resources.
  • Depot — extends your supply range. Wall it in with barricades!
  • Sniper — shoots zombies in range (needs ammo + survivors).
  • Barricade — blocks zombies; soaks hits using boards.

Zombies pour in from the bridges in escalating hordes. An undefended depot falls to a single zombie — its survivors flee to the nearest depot. Survive every wave and clear the city to win.
